Recipe Manager(Recipe Drive)

1. Upload Recipe - parse txt file with fetch() and import as recipe object. Save in localStorage 
2. Recipe Browser - browse recipe uploaded, folder view? list view? add Like, add tag, etc (like GDrive)
3. Recipe Display(viewer) - when recipe chosen in browser, view recipe in certain form. (pretty print)
4. Recipe Editor - open previously uploaded recipe and edit(as object) OR create blank recipe as a new one
5. Community uploaded Recipe - search recipes by other ppl! download recipe into user's local storage --> visible on recipe browser
-> similar to WP post page. 
6. Ingredients checkout - choose 1 or more recipe and organize all ingredients /equipments needed for reicpe (for shopping list)
7. What should i eat  - list view from Monday to Sunday, randomly pick 7 recipes and display them. (open in Recipe Browser)
